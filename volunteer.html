Volunteer With Us

Volunteers are the heart of The Giving Collective. Whether you have a few hours a week or can commit to a longer-term role, your time and talents make a real difference in our community. Join our team of dedicated volunteers and be part of something meaningful.

Why Volunteer?

Make an Impact

Your efforts directly improve the lives of individuals and families in our community, creating lasting positive change.

Build Skills

Gain valuable experience, develop new skills, and enhance your resume while giving back to your community.

Connect

Meet like-minded people, build meaningful relationships, and become part of a supportive community of changemakers.

Volunteer Opportunities

Program Support

Help with our educational programs, community events, and direct service delivery. Assist with tutoring, mentoring, event planning, and program facilitation.

Time Commitment: 4-8 hours per week

Administrative Support

Support our office operations with tasks like data entry, filing, reception, donor communications, and database management.

Time Commitment: Flexible, 2-4 hours per week

Outreach & Events

Help us spread the word about our work at community events, fundraisers, and awareness campaigns. Assist with event setup, registration, and coordination.

Time Commitment: Varies by event

Professional Services

Share your professional expertise in areas like marketing, IT, graphic design, legal services, accounting, or human resources to support our operations.

Time Commitment: Project-based, flexible

Youth Volunteers

Students aged 16+ can join our youth volunteer program, helping with community service projects and gaining valuable leadership experience.

Time Commitment: 2-4 hours per week
